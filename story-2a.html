After a long walk with your team, you decide to head back to the ship to set up your habitation pod.

When you wake up in the morning, you see a few large boulders near your pod that you hadn't noticed the day before.

As you eat breakfast, you hear a clank outside the pod. A minute later, you hear another. What could possibly be making that noise? You offer to take a look outside.

You step out the door, and what you see is unexplainable. A small rock lifts off the ground and flings itself at the pod.
