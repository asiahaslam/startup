Not knowing what else to do, you try speaking English to the creature. "We intend no harm. We did not know that there was life here. We have been looking for a safe place for our people to live," you say.

Remarkably, it seems to understand you. You hear a voice in your head that says, "We understand. We see that you are harmless. Your people can live on this planet under one condition: they must leave us alone under the ground, and you must not tell them that we are here. If these terms are not kept, know what we are capable of."
