You, Lucas, and Kristen arrive on the planet Arion. Your mission is to scope out the planet as a potential location for a human colony.

Arion is in the "Goldilocks zone" of its solar system, meaning it has water, breathable air, and a temperature and gravity level similar to that on Earth.

You disembark from the spaceship and take your first steps on the new planet. There is no sign of life anywhere. A thick layer of dust covers the surface, and you feel quite sure that it has never been disturbed.
