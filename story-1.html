Introduction

1 Nephi 3

1 And it came to pass that [my father] spake unto me, saying: Behold I have dreamed a dream, in the which the Lord hath commanded me that thou and thy brethren shall return to Jerusalem.

2 For behold, Laban hath the record of the Jews and also a genealogy of my forefathers, and they are engraven upon plates of brass

3 Wherefore, the Lord hath commanded me that thou and thy brothers should go unto the house of Laban, and seek the records, and bring them down hither into the wilderness.
