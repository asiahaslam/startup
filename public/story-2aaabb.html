Not knowing what else to do, the team tries thinking at the creature. "We intend no harm. We did not know that there was life here. We have been looking for a safe place for our people to live," they say.

Remarkably, the creature seems to understand them. They hear a voice in their heads that says, "We understand. We see that you are harmless. Your people can live on this planet under one condition: they must leave us alone under the ground, and you must not tell them that we are here. If these terms are not kept, know what we are capable of."
