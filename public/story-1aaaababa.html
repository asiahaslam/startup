Wearing their spacesuits, and Kristin disembark from the shuttle and step onto the rocky surface of the comet.

The sky—if you could call it that—is flickering green, as if it were filled with intense aurora borealis.

As inspects the ship, they find a thin layer of gray foam-like substance coating the metal. It is solid to the touch, as if it were made of iron.
